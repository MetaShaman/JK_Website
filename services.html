Comprehensive Land Stewardship & Project Management

I provide tailored land stewardship and project management services, designed to ensure the seamless operation, maintenance, and enhancement of your property. My approach integrates a deep understanding of both built and natural environments, focusing on long-term health and sustainable practices.

01

Structural Repairs & Maintenance Oversight

Ensuring the Longevity and Health of Your Structures

  • Keep an eye on the overall health of the property's buildings and systems, implementing proactive and preventative maintenance strategies.
  • Source, vet, and coordinate any needed repairs or upgrades with trusted contractors and vendors, leveraging my extensive experience in residential construction and design to ensure quality and efficiency.
  • Track maintenance needs and schedules so small issues don't become big ones, ensuring you're getting good value from all services.
Benefit: Avoid costly future repairs and ensure your property's infrastructure remains sound and valuable.
02

Landscape, Garden, and Land Projects

Cultivating Thriving, Regenerative Landscapes

  • Manage crews and timelines for ongoing landscaping and land stewardship work.
  • Support agricultural and permaculture efforts, including soil care, planting, composting, and irrigation, drawing on deep expertise as a garden and natural building educator.
  • Help set seasonal goals for the land and organize the right teams and tools to support them, providing guidance rooted in regenerative and ecological design principles.
Benefit: Transform your land into a vibrant, productive, and ecologically rich environment that flourishes year after year.
03

Design Support & Implementation Guidance

Bringing Your Vision to Life, Sustainably

  • Offer feedback and ideas on building or landscape designs, based on practical experience.
  • Help turn big ideas into step-by-step plans, sourcing materials and coordinating timelines.
  • Be your sounding board for choices around aesthetics, function, long-term ecological impact, and budget.
Benefit: Make informed design choices that blend aesthetics, function, and environmental responsibility, ensuring your vision is realized effectively.
04

Project Tracking & Ongoing Oversight

Seamless Progress, Complete Transparency

  • Set up simple tools (like a shared spreadsheet or Notion board) to track all active projects.
  • Monitor timelines, budgets, and open tasks, and provide weekly progress reports to keep you fully informed.
  • Help build out systems and routines that make the property easier to manage over time.
Benefit: Stay informed and confident that your projects are on track, on budget, and moving forward, without needing to manage the day-to-day details yourself.
05

Regulatory Compliance & Permitting

Navigating Regulations with Confidence

  • Proactively monitor and advise on local regulations, zoning requirements, and permitting needs relevant to property maintenance, improvements, and land use.
  • Assist in navigating the permitting process for various projects, ensuring compliance and smooth progression.
Benefit: Avoid delays and ensure all property improvements meet local requirements, saving you time and potential headaches.
