Meet Joon Kim: Your Partner in Property Stewardship

Professional Photo

With over 20 years of dedicated experience in project management and residential construction and design, I bring a unique blend of practical expertise and a deep understanding of ecological principles to property stewardship.

My background as a garden and natural building educator, coupled with extensive hands-on experience as a skilled craftsman across all areas of residential renovations, allows me to approach property care holistically.

I have a proven track record of managing large-scale agricultural projects and properties, from Atlanta, Georgia, to Northern California. This includes leading youth programs in natural earthen building and organic gardening, and facilitating community engagement at learning gardens. My work has even been featured in a documentary film on urban farming, underscoring my commitment to sustainable practices and community well-being.

Currently, I steward and manage a two-acre lush garden hillside in San Rafael, a testament to my ongoing passion for connecting with nature and fostering thriving environments. This personal dedication informs my professional approach, ensuring your property receives thoughtful, expert care aligned with both its practical needs and long-term vision.

My Philosophy

I believe that true property stewardship goes beyond mere maintenance; it's about fostering a dynamic relationship between the built environment and the natural world. My goal is to create spaces that are not only beautiful and functional but also ecologically resilient and aligned with the unique spirit of the land.
